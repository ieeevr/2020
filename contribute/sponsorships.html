IEEE VR 2020 - The 27th IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces

Important Dates

  • January 31, 2020: Deadline for application and full payment
  • February 14, 2020: Space assignment mailed to exhibitors
  • February 7, 2020: No refunds for cancellations

Overview

IEEE VR provides a unique opportunity for your organization to reach the world's foremost virtual reality researchers and application builders. We invite companies, government organizations, and academic institutions to showcase virtual reality related products – hardware, software, integrated systems, peripherals, innovations, or literature in one of our two dedicated exhibit areas.

Look forward to meeting with VR professionals eager to see what your company has to offer. Refreshments and a special reception will take place in the exhibit areas, ensuring good foot traffic to your display. Additional sponsorship and marketing opportunities are available. The Exhibitor and Sponsorship prospectus will be available soon.

Event Schedule At-a-Glance

  • March 22-23: Workshops, tutorials, and Doctoral Colloquium.
  • March 23- 24: Exhibitor Set-up (set-up ends at 11:0 AM on March 24)
  • March 24: Exhibitor’s Reception – Your first opportunity to meet and capture attention of all the conference attendees.
  • March 24-26: VR Conference Programs and Exhibition
